About Me

Input Image is a indie rock one man band steered by the song writer Philip Barton. Based in Newfoundland he plays any and every style making a broad musical landscape for you to explore in each album. Influences ranging from punk to poka he lives to tell stories.Wether its a love struck serial killer or a lonley man at the end of the world .He makes you feel like they are real and brings the strange subject matter down to something everybody can understand. Making his first album at the age of fourteen he always wrote songs. Entertaing house guest and church goers.So keep your ears opened and your hands glued to the dile cause strange things happen everyday and he's gonna tell you about them.
